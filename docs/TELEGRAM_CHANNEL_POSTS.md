## POST 5 — Order Management

```
📋 Order Management — From Submission to Delivery

Every order in MultiLang goes through a clear, trackable lifecycle:

📌 Stages of an Order:

⏳ Pending — Just received, awaiting review
💳 Payment Pending — Waiting for customer payment
✅ Payment Received — Payment uploaded/processed
🔎 Payment Confirmed — Manager verified payment
⚙️ In Progress — Translator is working
📦 Ready — Translation complete, awaiting pickup
🎉 Completed — Delivered to customer

At every stage, the system:
— Notifies the customer via Telegram bot
— Logs who changed the status and when
— Tracks the assigned staff member
— Keeps all uploaded files organized

Additional features:
📎 Multiple file uploads per order
⏰ Customer deadline requests
🏪 Walk-in (manual) orders for customers without Telegram
🔢 Sequential order numbers per center

Your team always knows what to work on next.
```
